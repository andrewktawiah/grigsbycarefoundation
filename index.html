Our Story

Building a legacy of service, love, and commitment to community

Minister Grigsby & Mrs. Eleanor Grigsby

For over 25 years, Minister Teklo Maxwell Grigsby II and his wife Eleanor have been partners in life and service. Together, they have dedicated themselves to improving their community through education initiatives, humanitarian outreach, and spiritual guidance.

Mrs. Eleanor Grigsby is an accomplished educator and philanthropist in her own right, having established several literacy programs and women's empowerment initiatives throughout Liberia. Her passion for education complements Minister Grigsby's legal and governmental expertise, creating a powerful force for positive change.

"We believe that service to others is the highest calling, and we are blessed to walk this path together," says Minister Grigsby. Their shared commitment to uplifting others has touched thousands of lives and continues to inspire those around them.
