About Atty. Grigsby

A Man of Law & Principle

Atty. Teklo Maxwell Grigsby is a distinguished lawyer with over 20 years of experience in corporate law, human rights advocacy, and public interest litigation. His unwavering commitment to justice has made him one of Liberia's most respected legal minds.

Beyond the courtroom, Atty. Grigsby is a devoted family man, married to Dr. Mrs. Grigsby, a renowned medical professional, and together they are proud parents to three children.

"Living for the sake of others is not just a motto, it's a way of life. Every day presents an opportunity to make a difference in someone's life."
